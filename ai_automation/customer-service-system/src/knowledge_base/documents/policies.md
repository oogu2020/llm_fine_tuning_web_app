# Company Policies

## Privacy Policy

### Data Collection

We collect only the data necessary to provide our services:
- Account information (name, email)
- Usage data (features accessed, time spent)
- Device information (browser type, OS)

### Data Protection

Your data is protected using:
- AES-256 encryption at rest
- TLS 1.3 for data in transit
- Regular security audits
- SOC 2 Type II certified

### Data Deletion

You can request complete data deletion:
- Via Settings > Data & Privacy > Delete Account
- Or email: privacy@company.com

Deletion is processed within 30 days and confirmed via email.

## Terms of Service

### Acceptable Use

Users must not:
- Violate any applicable laws
- Attempt to access unauthorized areas
- Share account credentials
- Use the service for illegal activities

### Service Availability

We aim for 99.9% uptime. Planned maintenance is announced 48 hours in advance.

### Liability Limitations

Our liability is limited to the amount paid for services in the past 12 months.

## Security Policy

### Reporting Security Issues

If you discover a security vulnerability:
1. Email security@company.com
2. Include detailed description
3. Allow 48 hours for response

We have a bug bounty program for confirmed security issues.

### Two-Factor Authentication

We strongly recommend enabling 2FA:
1. Go to Settings > Security
2. Click "Enable 2FA"
3. Follow the setup instructions

Supported methods: Authenticator apps, SMS, hardware keys

## Refund Policy

### Eligibility

Refunds may be granted for:
- Technical issues preventing use
- Accidental purchases
- Service unavailability > 24 hours

### Process

Refund requests:
1. Must be submitted within 14 days
2. Are processed within 5 business days
3. Return to original payment method

### Exceptions

No refunds for:
- Violations of Terms of Service
- Change of mind after 14 days
- Promotional or discounted purchases
