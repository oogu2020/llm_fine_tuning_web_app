# Frequently Asked Questions

## Account Issues

### How do I reset my password?

To reset your password:
1. Go to the login page
2. Click "Forgot Password"
3. Enter your email address
4. Check your email for reset link
5. Create a new password

If you don't receive the email within 5 minutes, check your spam folder or contact support.

### My account is locked. What should I do?

Accounts are typically locked after 5 failed login attempts. To unlock:
1. Wait 30 minutes and try again
2. Use the "Forgot Password" option if needed
3. Contact support if the issue persists

## Billing Questions

### How do I update my payment method?

You can update your payment method:
1. Log into your account
2. Go to Settings > Billing
3. Click "Update Payment Method"
4. Enter new card details

### When will I be charged?

Charges occur on the anniversary of your subscription date. You'll receive an email notification 3 days before the charge.

### How do I cancel my subscription?

To cancel:
1. Go to Settings > Subscription
2. Click "Cancel Subscription"
3. Follow the confirmation steps

Your access will continue until the end of the current billing period.

### Can I get a refund?

Refunds are considered on a case-by-case basis within 14 days of purchase. Contact support with your request.

## Technical Support

### The app is not loading

Try these steps:
1. Clear your browser cache
2. Try a different browser
3. Disable browser extensions temporarily
4. Check your internet connection

### I'm getting an error code

Please provide the exact error code when contacting support. Common codes:
- 404: Page not found - try refreshing
- 500: Server error - we're working on it
- 403: Permission denied - check your login status

### How do I export my data?

You can export your data:
1. Go to Settings > Data & Privacy
2. Click "Export Data"
3. Choose your preferred format (CSV, JSON, PDF)
4. Download will be emailed to you within 24 hours
